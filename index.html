PiggyRide Features

What we offer as a ride-sharing platform

Find piggyback rides

For passengers, PiggyRide help them find drivers who happen to be going to an adjacent destination are willing to offer a ride for a reasonable price.

Learn More

Offset inter-city travelling cost

For drivers, PiggyRide help them find passengers who need a ride to offset gas.

Learn More

Seamless matching and safe transaction

The AI powered algorithm of PiggyRide can help drivers and passengers seamlessly match. PiggyRide, as a third-party agent, ensures saft transaction.

About Us

Our Goal

The PiggyRide team strikes to cteate an alternative to ride-sharing for inter-city travellers aside from expensive Uber rides as well as slow and sometimes crowded train rides.

  • Faster
  • Cheaper
  • Safer

Our Technology

Blessed by the new advancement in AI and machine learning technology, PiggyRide is equiped with a smart recommender system and an inclusive user database. PiggyRide can learn users' preferences through using and adapt its results based on users' need.

  • Machine Learning
  • Big Data
  • Active User Profiling

Our Channels

PiggyRide will be made available on various platforms including HTML app, Android, IOS, facebook plugin and Wechat mini Programs

  • HTML App
  • Android and IOS App
  • Facebook Plugin and Wechat mini programs
